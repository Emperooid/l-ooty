Looty is more than a collaborative Arts collective

about looty

Created by Chidi Nwaubani in 2021, LOOTY is a dynamic fusion of creativity and technology that sparks conversations in the art world, Web3, and beyond. From reclaiming seized stolen artwork through innovative blockchain and NFT technology to crafting bespoke 3D experiences, LOOTY is pioneering new portals to enhance access and education. Empowered by Innovation and culture while embracing disruption, setting the stage for a future where art, tech,and imagination unite in unprecedented ways.

mission statement

Driven by provocative and daring interventions, LOOTY challenges the inherent hypocrisy of colonial, economic, political, and cultural systems. Committed to innovation, we continuously reimagine and redefine the digital sphere, ensuring every touchpoint reflects our core values and vision for a more equitable world
